One transported to hospital after accident on I-77

  • 6:40 pm
  • February 25, 2019
  • Top Stories

WVVA has confirmed with WV Turnpike Authority that a single vehicle crash happened around 4:24 p.m. on I-77 at mile marker 20. One person was transported to the hospital. WV State Police, Princeton Rescue Squad and Bluestone Valley Volunteer Fire Department all responded. The accident scene has been cleared. We will bring you updates as we learn more.
