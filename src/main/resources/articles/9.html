1 person taken to hospital after vehicle hits Sioux City building

  • 5:48 pm
  • February 25, 2019
  • Top Stories
Unmute
Remaining Ad Time Ad - 00:00

SIOUX CITY (KTIV) – A person was taken to a Sioux City hospital Monday afternoon after a vehicle hit a building.

Authorities were called to 11th and Court Street shortly before 5 p.m. Monday.

Authorities say when they arrived fire was coming from around the wheel well and the person was unresponsive.

Police say they believe a medical condition led to the accident.

The person was taken to a local hospital for further observation.

Alexander Keller, Sioux City Police Department, said the building and vehicle suffered minor damage, and that the snow banks and snow around the building stopped the vehicle from doing additional damage.

Travis Hoffer
