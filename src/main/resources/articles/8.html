Two transported to the hospital following shooting in North Charleston

February 25, 2019 at 9:38 PM EST - Updated February 26 at 12:26 AM

NORTH CHARLESTON, SC (WCSC) - Two people have been transported to the hospital following a shooting in North Charleston Monday night.

North Charleston police say officers are currently on the scene on Big Ben Court.

“Two adult victims were located suffering from gunshot wounds,” NCPD officials said."Both are being transported for treatment."

Investigators say there is no suspect information at this time, and police are continuing the investigation.
